Dark Matter Studio was founded by Tara Paglino — a creative strategist, product leader, and bioinformatist who has spent nearly a decade building AI-driven products at Tempus AI, PathAI, and Peloton, and conducting research at the Sean N. Parker Lab at Stanford University.

In physics, dark matter is the invisible substance that holds galaxies together — undetectable, yet responsible for everything. We believe the same is true of creative and product strategy: the invisible infrastructure that determines whether a company's science gets the recognition, funding, and talent it deserves.

We specialize in AI, longevity, oncology, and precision health — spaces where the science is profound and the storytelling and product thinking usually are not. We fix both.
